The main goal of Run Dos Run! is to joy an experience. There's no win time or score, just an space to chill and relax out of the frenetic modern life style. Please, take a sit and free your mind.

This game was created for academic purposes by Rubén Sospedra.

The main framework is Phaser v1.6

The full rights of the music corresponds to Kabanjak and the Ancient Astronatus. The title of the track is Gypsy Soul.
